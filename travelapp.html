Travel App

In the first semester of this school year, we had to make our own travel app. It had to be a multidevice app that helps the user before, during and / or after the trip.
My app is called Zone. The app helps the user to find local places and activities wherever he is located. It uses geocoding, so it automatically knows where you are if you have your location turned on.
Each place has a description, the directions, and a like button. In the app you can also store all your important travel documents. And if you're in a foreign country with others, you can invite them, start a chat, and easily share and compare interesting places, and easily share your location with them.
